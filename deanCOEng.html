Dean of College of Engineering

An important leadership role inside a university or college is the Dean of the College of Engineering, who is in charge of directing and supervising the academic and research initiatives of the organization's engineering departments. This person works closely with faculty, staff, students, and outside partners to ensure that the College's programs and activities achieve the highest standards of quality. They also provide strategic direction and leadership for the College. The Dean of the College of Engineering is accountable for making sure that the College's academic programs are created to give students a cutting-edge education that prepares them for prosperous careers in engineering. The Dean reports to the Provost or Vice President for Academic Affairs. In order to create and implement cutting-edge curricula, the dean collaborates closely with department heads and faculty.
