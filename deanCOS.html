Dean of College of Science

The academic and research activities of the institution's science departments are supervised and managed by the dean of the college of science, a top administrative position within a university or college. This person is in charge of organizing a group of academic staff, support personnel, and students to carry out the academic programs, research projects, and strategic goals of the college. The Dean of the College of Science often works closely with other deans and administrators to accomplish the mission and objectives of the organization and reports directly to the Provost or Vice President for Academic Affairs. The academic programs at the College must be of the greatest caliber and suit the needs of the community, the faculty, and the students, according to the Dean.
