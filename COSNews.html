College of Science News and Events

February 20, 2023 - February 23, 2023

College of Science Week was held in URSM Quadrangle

The COS week, or college of science week, took place from February 20–23, 2023, when students in the department of science had no classes for a week. But there was an event where students could enjoy it. At the opening of the event, the students held a meeting in the URSM quadrangle and waited for the others to start the parade of COS Week as the event opened. Students in the college of science can play and join a variety of sports games and contests, such as the 'Ginoo at Binibining Agham" (GBA), Bench Yell, and eSports, which are the battles of different courses in the college of science. There are also some booths for food, picture taking in the photo booth, and other things you can buy, such as accessories provided by the students in the college of science.

February 23, 2023

Ginoo at Binibining Agham was held in EARTS

On February 23, 2023, the GBA will host "Ginoo at Binibining Agham," the prestigious pageant in COS week for the department of science. Where is the one who claimed the throne as Ginoo at Binibining Agham? Ladies and gentlemen, this year's Ginoo at Binibining Agham has finally found the diamonds of the season! And they're none other than, Mr. Ferdinando Rafanan of BS Mathematics, and Ms. Geralyn Mae Lastica of BS Computer Science. 1st Runners Up: Mr. Kian Louis Garcia and Ms Nicole Tañega 2nd Runners Up: Mr. Angelico Quitalig and Ms. Catherine Sanchez

(Source: URSM Ginoo at binibining agham)
