Student Organization of College of Science

Technology Innovators Trough Programming and Algorithm Sciences

The Technology Innovators Trough Programming and Algorithm Sciences is student organization in Computer Science that implore a a platform for members to collaborate, learn, and share knowledge. Our events, workshops, and projects aim to broaden technical expertise and promote diversity and inclusion within the field. We encourage lifelong learning and the use of technology for positive impact.

The Essence

We, the sovereign students of the University Of Rizal System, Morong Campus- College of Science, imploring the aid of Almighty God, aware of the need to establish a genuine student publication that shall embody our ideals and aspirations, promote and protect our general interest, cultivate unity and cooperation in all sectors of community, uphold our rights and welfare and advance our national consciousness based on the fundamental justice, freedom and democracy, do hereby ordain this Constitution and By-Laws.

Math Circle

We the member of College of Science Math Circle having been organized promulgate and adopt this constitution in order to upgrade the Mathematics abilities of the student of the College of Science, develop the leadership potentials of BS Math students and poster camaraderie among the students to hereby from this organization.

College of Science Athletic Association

Our student organization promotes physical fitness and healthy lifestyles among College of Science students through sports events, tournaments, and fitness programs. We also encourage leadership development and community service through athletic activities.

Association of Psychology Students

Imploring the aid of almighty God, in order to join ourselves into an organization that aims to promote psychological awareness and shall embody our ideals, protect our psychological interest, cultivate unity and cooperation among members of the academics, do hereby ordain and promulgate this constitution.

Biology Students Association

Acts as a forum for these students to voice their opinions and concerns and serves as a bridge between the Department of Biological Sciences and the students.

Complete Guidelines and Guides

Enhanced the knowledge of the students particularly BGP Community through active participation in local fairs, contest in other activities and Promote the educational advancement through intellectual and social activities. Foster the involvement of the students in upgrading the quality of College of Science education and in improving its atmosphere for learning.

College of Science Student Body

We, the members of College of Science Student Body (COSSB) imploring the aid of Almighty God, in order to establish an organization that shall embody the vision, mission, goals and objectives of the College of Science and of the University as a whole to become the premier technology driven, higher education institution and to produce value- laden graduates in different field of specialization, at the same time, develop talents, skill, social conscience, dedicate leadership, honor excellence and enlighten nationalism, do ordain and promulgate this constitution.
