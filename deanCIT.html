Dean of College of Industrial Technology

The Dean of the College of Industrial Technology is a respected academic leader who is responsible for overseeing the development and implementation of the college's strategic plans and policies. This individual serves as the chief administrator and spokesperson for the college and works closely with faculty, staff, students, and external stakeholders to ensure that the college is providing high-quality educational programs that meet the needs of industry and society. The Dean of the College of Industrial Technology is typically an accomplished scholar and educator who has significant experience in the field of industrial technology. They are responsible for managing the day-to-day operations of the college, including budgeting, personnel management, and program development. Additionally, the Dean is often involved in fundraising and community outreach efforts, helping to secure funding and partnerships that can support the college's mission and vision. As an advocate for the college and its students, the Dean works to foster a positive and inclusive learning environment that encourages innovation, collaboration, and critical thinking. They also play an important role in promoting research and scholarship within the college, working to attract and retain talented faculty and students who are committed to advancing the field of industrial technology. Overall, the Dean of the College of Industrial Technology is a critical leader within the academic community, responsible for shaping the future of the college and its students while advancing the state of knowledge in the field of industrial technology.
