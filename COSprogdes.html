College of Science Programs

      The College of Science is an academic division dedicated to the study of natural sciences, providing students with the opportunity to gain a deep understanding of scientific principles, theories, and methodologies. Through coursework, laboratory research, and field experiences, students develop critical thinking skills, problem-solving abilities, and a foundation of knowledge that can be applied to many career paths.
