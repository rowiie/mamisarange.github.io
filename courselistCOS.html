Program Offerings

Bachelor of Science in Biology

A Bachelor of Science in Biology is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on the study of living organisms and their interactions with their environment. Students take lecture-based courses, laboratory experiments, and field studies to gain a deep understanding of life sciences.

Bachelor of Science in Human Services

Students who complete a Bachelor of Human Services program will have the knowledge and abilities necessary to work in a variety of positions that entail assisting people and communities in need. It incorporates curriculum in psychology, sociology, public health, counseling, social work, and counseling. Graduates can work in social services, nonprofits, community development, public health, and government organizations, among other fields.

Bachelor of Science in Psychology

A Bachelor of Science in Psychology program's curriculum often includes courses in cognitive psychology, social psychology, developmental psychology, abnormal psychology, research methodologies, and statistics. Through lab work, research projects, and internships, students may also have the chance to gain practical experience.

Bachelor of Science in Mathematics

The Bachelor of Science in Mathematics is an undergraduate degree that enables students to gain a thorough understanding of mathematical theory, computational methods, and problem-solving abilities. Along with learning about topics like probability theory, calculus, linear algebra, discrete mathematics, and statistics in class, students also get hands-on training in computer programming, mathematical modeling, and numerical analysis.

Bachelor of Science in Human Services

A bachelor's degree program in computer science focuses on the investigation of computers, computing systems, and computational procedures. Computer programming, algorithms, data structures, computer organization and architecture, software engineering, database systems, operating systems, computer networks, and artificial intelligence are all covered in this program's courses. Through projects, internships, and laboratory work, program participants also acquire practical experience.
