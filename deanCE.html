Dean of College of Education

The academic and research activities of an institution's education departments are supervised and managed by the dean of the college of education, a top administrative position within a university or college. This person is in charge of providing leadership and guidance to make sure that the College's initiatives and programs uphold the highest standards of excellence and equip students with the skills they need to pursue successful careers in education. The Dean, who works closely with department chairmen, professors, staff, and outside partners to develop and execute cutting-edge curriculum, research programs, and cooperative collaborations that advance the College's mission and goals, reports to the Provost or Vice President for Academic Affairs. The Dean needs to have good interpersonal, leadership, and communication skills as well as a thorough knowledge of education research in order to succeed in this position.
